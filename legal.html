Legal & data handling

The fine print, plainly

Last updated: June 2026
CanLah assists — it does not replace a qualified person. Every output (bills of quantities, tree/traffic extractions, schedules, compliance notes) is AI-generated and may contain errors or omissions. A competent QS, engineer, or PE must review and verify everything before it is used for tender, submission, costing, or any decision with legal or financial weight.

1. AI output is a draft, not advice

CanLah reads construction documents with a large language model and returns structured drafts. It is not engineering, legal, surveying, or professional advice. Quantities, measurements, classifications and dates are estimates derived from your source files and can be wrong — especially with poor scans, handwriting, ambiguous drawings, or non-standard formats.

  • Always cross-check figures against the original document.
  • Do not submit AI output to authorities (BCA, SCDF, NParks, LTA, NEA, MOM) without a qualified person's review and sign-off.
  • CanLah is not liable for losses arising from reliance on unverified output.

2. How your documents are handled

  • Upload & processing. Files you upload are sent to our processing function and to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate the analysis. Large files are uploaded directly to encrypted blob storage.
  • Transient by default. The uploaded file is removed from blob storage once it has been handed to the model for analysis; it is not kept as a permanent copy.
  • Saved reports. If you save a report, the extracted result (not the original file) is stored in our database against your account so you can retrieve it later. You can delete saved reports.
  • AI provider. Document contents are processed by Anthropic under their API terms. We do not use your documents to train models.
  • No selling of data. We do not sell your documents or the data extracted from them.

3. Accounts & access

During the pilot, accounts are created by our team — there is no open sign-up. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential and for activity under your account. Tell us immediately if you suspect unauthorised access.

4. Your responsibilities

  • Only upload documents you are authorised to share.
  • Do not upload personal data you don't need processed.
  • Use the output as a starting point — verify before you rely on it.

5. PDPA & data requests

We handle personal data in line with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). To ask what we hold, request a correction, or request deletion of your account and saved reports, contact our data point-of-contact below. We aim to respond within a reasonable time.

Data & privacy contact: hello@canlah.ai
